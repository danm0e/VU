About us

Founded in 2013 by a team of industry experts and entrepreneurs, including Charles Oliver II with 54 years of aviation business experience and Jahid Fazal-Karim with over 25 years of corporate and business aviation experience.

  • Vu Systems is an affiliate of Jetcraft Corporation, one of the world’s largest aircraft brokers.
